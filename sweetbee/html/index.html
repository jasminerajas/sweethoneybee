Real, Raw American
Honey from Florida

We’re bee-keepin’, hand-shakin’, promise-keepin’ farmers from the little town of Sorrento, Florida. At the Sweet Bee Company, we treat our bees like family, and our finger-lickin’ honey proves it.

We pride ourselves on producing 100% raw, unfiltered honey from the 1,200 beehives on our six-acre farm. There’s no filtering, heating or funny stuff. It’s just all-natural, pure Florida honey straight from our beehives to your kitchen table.

We proudly harvest natural wild honey flavors, including orange blossom, gallberry, summer wildflower, winter wildflower and many more. We produce bee pollen, royal jelly and bee venom.

Our beehives can be safely transported to pollinate different plants. We also specialize in ethical bee removals.

The Health Benefits of Honey

Real Honey,
Really Good for You

It’s truly quite amazzzzzzing that something so delicious can be so healthy for you. Our raw, unfiltered honey is jam-packed with antioxidants that protect your body from cell damage due to free radicals.

Honey, ooey-gooey with antibacterial and anti-fungal properties, helps to treat burns and heal wounds.
